Revolution Computer Services provides various services in the field of PC repair and OS optimization in the Kitchener-Waterloo region. Please note that while I am located in interning in San Francisco, California, I will only be able to offer some remote services. I will be returning to Waterloo in December 2014.

System Repair

Our system repair service diagnoses any problem you may be having with your computer, device, or network. We will then provide advice on how to solve the problem, or provide further service in fixing the issue for you.

Cost: $30/hr


System Reformat

Our system reformat service formats your hard drive, and then we will install an operating system of your choice.

Cost: $60*

* Plus cost of operating system (if applicable). Choose to save your undamaged files for an additional $15.

Virus Removal

Our virus removal service removes any viruses, spyware, or malware that may be hidden on your computer to improve performance in your system.

Cost: $40

System Clean-Up

Our system clean-up service provides tweaking of various aspects of your computer (as well as the operating system) to speed up boot loading time and improve general system performance. Our system clean-up service includes registry cleaning.

Cost: $40


Data Recovery

Our data recovery service attempts to recover all possible data from a damaged or corrupt storage device (usually a hard drive).

NOTICE: Often damaged or corrupted devices will contain files that are unrecoverable. Revolution Computer Services is not responsible for being unable to recover data that is reasonably unrecoverable.

Cost: $60†

† Plus cost of hard drives (if applicable).
