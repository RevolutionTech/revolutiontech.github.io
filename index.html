My name is Lucas Connors. I attend the University of Waterloo where I study computer science with a minor in cognitive science. I am passionate about creating well-structured and efficient programs that are simple to understand and easy to use. I love learning new things from different people. I think understanding how people live, work and play is the first step to making their world a better place, one line of code at a time.

The initial sparks of Revolution Technologies began in 2003 when I started creating computer games, eventually under the name Revolution Games. In 2008, I expanded it to also include multimedia productions, software programs, and computer repair. Although progress on some of my projects has suffered while attending university, I continue to help customers in the Kitchener-Waterloo region with their computer-related issues.
